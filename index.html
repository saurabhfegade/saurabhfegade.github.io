About Us

We are a Sports Medicine & Management company run by a team of Sports Medicine Doctors. Kinspire Sports Clinic was born on the grounds of providing a holistic treatment which works in unison with other disciplines for the betterment of an individual.

As the name suggests, Kinspire comes from Kinesis and Inspiration. We are Movement (Kinesis) Inspiration Facilitators. Under one roof, you have the expertise of our Sports Physician, Physiotherapist, Strength and Conditioning Coach, Sports Massage Therapist and Aerial Yoga experts. We are not strictly conventional in our approach but we do focus on keeping you on your toes. LITERALLY..!!

Why Us

Sports, especially those requiring high amount of team dynamics and involving movements of a higher intensity render the athletes vulnerable to a plethora of injuries and overtraining. A dedicated and scientifically sound and experienced medical team on and off the field ensures that every athlete in the team is up and ready to perform from the word "go".
While a physiotherapist will ensure quality care and injury prevention, fitness trainer will help solidify the foundational human movements that a physiotherapist corrects using various training methodologies. Also, sports massage therapist will work in conjunction with the physiotherapist and trainer to ensure full and focused recovery of every individual athlete.

At Kinspire, we believe that our ability to keep every athlete in their prime condition using evidence-based approach will help athletes understand their bodies better, thereby helping them to keep moving and performing the right way. We believe that if an athlete can move right then he can live better and will perform at his maximum potential!
