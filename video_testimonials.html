Mr. Sachin

“Last Thursday while doing a speed run I had a hamstring pull” We at Kinspire take such things as a challenge. People train all year round and sometimes due to some incidents the possibilities of them successfully completing the Marathon are almost zero. When a runner like Mr. Sachin with such a genuine passion comes to us for recovery, we put in all our efforts to make sure he runs the race successfully. For most of these people it’s not about being their personal best or winning these races. It’s just about being a part of the event which they have dreamt of and waited all year long. Mr. Sachin is an avid marathon runner who has been a marathon runner for many years and was one of the first runners to reach at the recovery zone.

Ms. Moushmi Thakur
